# Mobile Fitness App

## Overview

Designed and developed a cross-platform fitness tracking application with personalized workout plans, nutrition tracking, and social features. Built with React Native to provide a native experience on both iOS and Android.

## Key Features

### Workout Tracking
- Pre-built workout routines for different fitness levels
- Custom workout builder
- Exercise library with video demonstrations
- Progress tracking and statistics
- Rest timer and interval training support
- Form tips and safety guidelines

### Nutrition Management
- Calorie and macro tracking
- Barcode scanner for food logging
- Meal planning and recipes
- Water intake tracking
- Integration with nutrition databases
- Custom food entries

### Progress Monitoring
- Weight and body measurements tracking
- Progress photos with timeline
- Strength progression charts
- Cardio performance metrics
- Achievement badges and milestones
- Weekly and monthly reports

### Social Features
- Follow friends and share workouts
- Community challenges
- Leaderboards and competitions
- Workout sharing
- Motivational feed
- Private messaging

## Technical Stack

- **Frontend**: React Native, TypeScript
- **State Management**: Redux Toolkit
- **Navigation**: React Navigation
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express
- **Database**: MongoDB
- **Authentication**: Firebase Auth
- **Storage**: AWS S3 for media
- **Push Notifications**: Firebase Cloud Messaging
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics, Mixpanel

## Design Philosophy

### User-Centric Design
- Conducted user research with 100+ fitness enthusiasts
- Created personas for different user types
- Iterative prototyping and testing
- Focus on motivation and habit formation

### Accessibility
- VoiceOver/TalkBack support
- High contrast mode
- Adjustable text sizes
- Color-blind friendly palette
- Haptic feedback

## Performance Optimizations

- Lazy loading for workout videos
- Image compression and caching
- Offline-first architecture
- Background sync for data
- Optimized animations (60fps)
- Battery-efficient location tracking

## Challenges & Solutions

**Challenge**: Keeping users motivated long-term
**Solution**: Implemented gamification, streaks, and social accountability features

**Challenge**: Accurate calorie tracking
**Solution**: Integrated multiple nutrition databases and ML-based food recognition

**Challenge**: Battery drain from GPS tracking
**Solution**: Smart location updates and activity recognition
