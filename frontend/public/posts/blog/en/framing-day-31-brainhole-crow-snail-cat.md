# Brainhole Crow and Snail Cat

*Published on February 8, 2021*

There are two creatures in my mind that often argue. When one speaks, the other refutes. When I was a child, they were two colored ghosts. As I grew older, they differentiated into four different manga character images. Now I can finally pull them out of my mind and make them concrete.

## Brainhole Crow

A crow with brainholes as its eyes. An arrogant creature that likes to stand on telephone poles, sometimes complaining and expressing dissatisfaction. It has wild, unconstrained imagination, always viewing everything from a third-person perspective. It loves logical and challenging things, and doesn't spend much time understanding new things, but it's impatient, usually just observing for a while before flying away.

## Snail Cat

A cat that can curl its tail into a shell. A timid creature that likes to curl up in its shell reading books. It moves slowly and retreats into its shell to avoid uncertainty. It hates being gazed at. It is earnest with a bit of slowness, insensitive to the outside world, has exceptional memory, loves appreciating art, has a deep-diving exploratory spirit, but usually has no concept of time.
