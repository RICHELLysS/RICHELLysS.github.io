# Numerology

*Published on March 1, 2021*

At 7:06 AM, I started writing this. The purpose of writing this is to think about how I will spend the day. A day has 24 hours, 48 half-hours. Every second passes, and I don't know what will happen in the next second or what I want to do. Treat the 48 half-hours as a linked list from 0 to 47. This entry is 0. Trying to fill every slot with tasks is too troublesome, too brain-consuming, too rigid. So I set sleep time as 14 slots, work time divided into 4, 4, 6, and learning/entertainment as 12 and 8. Since I don't have a job yet, this is the allocation for now. If I get a job, I'll swap the two. For every slot completed ahead of schedule, the remaining time is used to think about what to do in the next slot.

It's now 7:18 AM. The rules are set. Game begins.
