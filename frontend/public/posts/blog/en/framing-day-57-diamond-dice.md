# Diamond Dice

*Published on July 30, 2021*

Assume "I" am the player of this world. When "I" am confused, "I" particularly need a "person" to tell me what to do next. This "person" cannot interfere with "my" decisions, because "I" only need advice. At this time, the diamond dice play the role of giving advice.

Initially, set 11 dice in a plastic box. "I" think of a question (or think of nothing at all), close the box, shake it randomly, then open it. Example result: 4 learning tools, 1 move forward, 2 intersection and conflict, 1 establish relationship, 3 converters. I can understand that the dice suggest that in this phase, I put 4 drive into studies, 3 into adjusting my state, 2 into intersection and conflict, 1 into establishing relationships, 1 into moving forward.

The beauty of this rule is that the dice rule itself is created by "me." The meaning of each pattern is also defined and understood by "me." The quantities are also determined by "me." Only the shaking phase is given to "chaos." That is, "I" have achieved, to some degree of metaphysics, "me" giving advice to "myself." Then "I" can see a unique path forward in this time and space.
