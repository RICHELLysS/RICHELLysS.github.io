# Separation, Reconciliation, and Energy Release

*Published on May 14, 2021*

Recently I seem to have realized something very profound, but truly profound things are actually very simple. To summarize: everything can be described by separation and reconciliation.

Start with "me." "I" am a person who believes anything is possible. If "I" corresponds to white, then the person opposite me is someone who, under the system I define, believes nothing is possible.

Definition done. Now apply a case to illustrate. Simulate a scene: at a certain time, a certain place, a certain person dies in a certain way. This event corresponds to white. The event spreads at the speed of light and is received by a certain platform (note: "platform" can also refer to a human brain). This platform generates an interpretation of "white," which corresponds to "black." Now the platform has two choices: "reconciliation" or "energy release." "Reconciliation" means seeking the truth of the event and disseminating information accurately and precisely. "Energy release" means manufacturing false information for profit.

Based on the choice, "black" can be divided into two types: "reconciliation" corresponds to white, "energy release" corresponds to black. The platform's choice determines the risks it will face in the future.

Simulate two paths: 1) For some reason, the platform chooses "energy release." Energy release includes but is not limited to: gaining profit, gaining attention, mobilizing group emotions, attempting to attack or destroy another group. This model can be mapped to conflict events. Usually, the biggest beneficiaries are platforms that spread false information—it's easy to see what kind of people these profit-makers are. 2) For some reason, the platform chooses "reconciliation." Reconciliation includes but is not limited to: tracing the origin of the event, collecting credible evidence, drawing high-probability conclusions, reporting the event accurately. Platforms that get to the bottom of the nature and process of events and disclose facts rather than inciting the masses—it's easy to see what kind of people these contributors are.

Further subdivision is possible. For example, a platform that chooses "energy release" might just want attention but unintentionally causes a disaster, or it might genuinely want to incite opposition but its clumsy tricks lack influence. A platform that chooses "reconciliation" might just want to clarify things but uses inappropriate wording causing unnecessary misunderstandings, or brings too much personal emotion, becoming a platform that incites the masses. In short, when an event occurs, it's like catching a wave frequency. After various platforms' "reconciliation" and "energy release," it will eventually drop from "high attention" to "low attention." Platforms begin waiting for the next wave, like a wave with a specific frequency, cycling through different surface events with the same essence. As long as a platform catches the crest of a wave, its fate changes.

For some reason, I've been sensing 24 and 5 very strongly lately. Perhaps it's the interaction between time and the human heart. Maybe this is a time node. After all, in the He Tu Luo Shu, 5 is at the center. A new cycle seems to be beginning (what am I talking about?).
