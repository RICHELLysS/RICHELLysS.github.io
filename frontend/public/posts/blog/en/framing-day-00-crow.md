# Crow

*Published on December 17, 2019*

The feeling of decadence is back again. How long did I persist in doing something last time? Can't stand still! At least think about something substantial every day; otherwise, dopamine always makes decisions before my brain, and I'll never reach my goals. If the purpose of the previous forty-plus days was to unify my personality, then the purpose of this record is to learn rational planning and adapt to the anti-comfort zone. As a crow, I can't become the phoenix that all birds expect—so I'll just be an ordinary excellent crow. Today is December 17, 2019, the Zeroth Framing Day.
